Verfiying your Number

Requirements

 1. You can only verify a phone number you own. 2. You must be able to receive phone calls and SMS to the phone number you are trying to verify. 3.You must have a working Internet connection through mobile data or WiFi.

How to Verify

 1. Enter your phone number in the box on the right. Don't put any 0 before your phone number. 2. Tap Next to request a code. 3. Enter the 6-digit code you receive via SMS.
If you didn't receive the 6-digit code by SMS 1. Wait for the progress bar to finish and retry. 2.The wait may take up to 10 minutes.
